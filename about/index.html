About

EverGreen Empire is a global music and creative powerhouse, built to protect artists, expand their reach, and reward creators. Every sound, every idea, and every creator matters to us. We are more than a label... we are a creative family shaping the future of music.

EverGreen Empire combines vision, innovation, and care, creating a complete ecosystem where artists thrive, creators are rewarded, and music reaches the world.

Our Divisions

Specialized platforms working together to support artists and creators

EverHub

Our media and storytelling division. EverHub shares artist news, label updates, catalog highlights, and connects directly with our community. Stay informed about the latest developments and behind-the-scenes stories from the Empire.

EverNet

Our YouTube-focused creator rewards platform. EverNet empowers creators by paying them for premium usage views, giving real value for their creativity. We believe every creator deserves to be compensated for their impact and influence.

Our Mission

To build a sustainable creative ecosystem where artists maintain control, creators receive fair compensation, and music transcends boundaries to inspire global audiences. We're committed to innovation, integrity, and the infinite potential of human creativity.
