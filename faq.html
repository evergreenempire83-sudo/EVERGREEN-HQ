General Company Information

What does EverGreen Empire do?

For Artists & Creators

Who do I contact if I have questions about royalty claims, payouts, or administration?

Email our support team at support@evergreenempireltd.com for any questions regarding payouts, Content ID, or account details.

How do I submit my music to EverGreen Empire?

Artists can submit their music through Ever Hub using our official submission form. All submissions are reviewed by our team.

I received an email from someone claiming to be an EverGreen Empire representative requesting payment. Is this legitimate?

Never make payments unless instructed through our official channels (info@evergreenempireltd.com or support@evergreenempireltd.com). Always verify authenticity with our support team.
