This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information in connection with your use of our websites, online and offline services, and emails ("Site" or "Sites"). Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information when you interact with our Sites, including:

  • Personal information: name, email, phone number, mailing address
  • Usage information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior
  • Creator/Artist information: music submissions, account information, payment details

When this Privacy Policy uses the term "personal information", we mean information that identifies or can reasonably identify a particular individual, such as full name, street address, telephone number, email address, or other identifiers.

Information collected on the Sites may be used by EverGreen Empire, our labels, agents, subsidiaries, trusted partners, and affiliates acting on our behalf (collectively, "EverGreen Empire") in the manner described below.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Sites and services
  • Process music submissions and Creator/Artist applications
  • Facilitate payments and royalties for artists and creators
  • Send updates, newsletters, and marketing communications
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
3

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Our trusted service providers and partners who assist in operating the Sites or delivering services
  • Legal authorities, when required by law
  • Other affiliates of EverGreen Empire for business and operational purposes

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and other tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance your experience on our Sites
  • Measure engagement and performance of content
  • Deliver targeted communications or marketing

You may manage or disable cookies via your browser settings.

5

Data Security

We implement technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

6

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request restrictions on processing of your information

To exercise your rights, contact support@evergreenempireltd.com.

7

Children's Privacy

Our Sites and services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The last updated date will always appear at the top. We encourage you to review it periodically.
