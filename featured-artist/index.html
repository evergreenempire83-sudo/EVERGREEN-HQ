IAkeys
Contemporary R&B

IAkeys (born December 19, 1999) is a Nigerian recording artist, music executive, and entrepreneur, one of the emerging independent figures in African music in the early 21st century. Israel Abel grew up in Taraba, where he was raised with a deep passion for both music and business. His early experiences shaped the vision of independence and ownership that would define his career. Performing under the name IAkeys X, he began releasing original music that blended R&B, Afro-soul, and cinematic soundscapes. His debut album, Fallen Wings, introduced him to a wider audience, followed by the EP Dreams Across Realities and singles such as Paper Wall and Climb.

Ande Wealth
Afro Gospel

Ande Wealth is a groundbreaking Afro Gospel artist who fuses traditional African rhythms with contemporary gospel messages, creating a unique sound that transcends cultural and generational boundaries. Born in Nigeria's vibrant musical landscape, Ande discovered his calling early, using his powerful vocals to bridge the gap between spiritual devotion and modern musical expression. His ministry through music has touched thousands, with hits like "Paranoid" and "Hold my Hands" becoming anthems in worship circles across Africa.

JOE ED
American R&B

JOE ED is a soulful American R&B sensation whose velvet vocals and heartfelt lyrics have captivated audiences worldwide. Born and raised in Chicago's vibrant music scene, Joe Edward "JOE ED" Davidson honed his craft in local jazz clubs before breaking into the mainstream with his debut EP "Midnight Conversations." His music seamlessly blends classic R&B sensibilities with contemporary production, creating a signature sound that pays homage to legends like Luther Vandross while pushing the genre forward. With hit singles like "City Lights"Break Apart" gaining millions of streams, JOE ED has established himself as one of R&B's most promising new voices, known for his electrifying live performances and authentic storytelling.

Eliot Blaze
Emotional & Aggressive

Eliot Blaze is the raw, unfiltered voice of a generation, channeling intense emotion and fiery passion into every performance. Known for his cathartic vocal delivery that oscillates between vulnerable whispers and explosive crescendos, Eliot creates musical experiences that feel both deeply personal and universally resonant. His aggressive yet controlled approach to songwriting tackles themes of heartbreak, resilience, and personal transformation with unapologetic honesty. Emerging from London's underground music scene, Eliot's breakout single "Cruel Life" became an anthem for those navigating emotional turmoil. His live shows are legendary for their emotional intensity, often leaving audiences both drained and transformed. Eliot represents the beautiful chaos of human emotion, proving that sometimes the most powerful healing comes from embracing the fire within.
